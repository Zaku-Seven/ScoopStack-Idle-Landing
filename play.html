How to Play

  1. Tap the cone to drop a scoop, then tap again as each one lands to build a combo for bonus coins.
  2. Fill the cone and it sells automatically for coins. Rarer flavors are worth more.
  3. Spend coins in the Shop on upgrades. Grab the Auto-scooper early so cones sell themselves, even while you're away.
  4. Keep earning to unlock new flavors, then tap the bar up top to travel to new worlds with bigger payouts.
  5. When growth slows, open Franchise to reset for permanent Stars that make every new run faster.
